How to Take Your Own Photos

Document your own transformation with these professional tips:

Equipment Setup

  • Lighting: Use ring light at 6-inch distance for even illumination
  • Camera: iPhone with 2× lens (or Android equivalent)
  • Settings: Use native camera app, disable filters
  • Positioning: Maintain consistent distance and angle

Standardized Process

  1. Start with freshly cleansed, product-free skin
  2. Photograph at the same time of day in each session
  3. Maintain neutral expression across all photos
  4. Capture front, both sides, and oblique angles
  5. Include a coin or ruler in initial photos for reference

Important Considerations Before Trying

Not recommended if you have:

  • Active acne breakouts (can spread bacteria)
  • Sunburned or irritated skin
  • History of keloid scarring
  • Active eczema, psoriasis, or rosacea flares

Safety First:

  • Patch test before use: Apply spray to inner arm and wait 24 hours
  • Results are temporary—vellus hairs typically regrow within 2-4 weeks
  • Always use a clean, sharp blade to prevent irritation
  • Follow with a gentle, fragrance-free moisturizer
