Shelf Life & Expiration

Like most cosmetic products, hair identifier sprays have a limited shelf life:

  • Unopened: 2-3 years (check manufacturer's date)
  • After opening: 12-18 months (varies by brand)
  • Signs of expiration: Change in color, consistency, or scent; decreased effectiveness

To extend shelf life, store your spray in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Avoid bathroom storage where humidity and temperature fluctuations can degrade the formula faster.

When to Replace Your Spray

Knowing when to replace your hair identifier spray ensures optimal results and skin safety:

  • Empty bottle: Obviously, when the product is completely used
  • Changed consistency: If the spray becomes watery, clumpy, or separated
  • Altered scent: If it develops an unusual or unpleasant odor
  • Decreased effectiveness: If it no longer highlights hairs properly
  • Reached expiration date: Most brands recommend replacement after 18 months
